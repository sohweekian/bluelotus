§ 06 — APPLICATION PROTOCOL

The Application Ritual

Preparation

Cleanse the face and neck thoroughly. Pat dry — do not rub. Dispense 3–4 drops (adult) or 2 drops (elderly/sensitive skin) onto the fingertips. Rub the fingertips together gently for 5 seconds to warm the serum — this activates the aromatic compounds and accelerates absorption. Inhale gently from the fingertips before application to allow the olfactory-limbic pathway to begin activation.

Application Technique

Using the press-and-lift method — never drag or rub — apply the serum across the face beginning at the forehead, moving to cheeks, nose, chin, and finally the neck. Give particular attention to the neck — use upward strokes, pressing gently to encourage lymphatic movement and deeper penetration. Allow 60–90 seconds for initial absorption.
