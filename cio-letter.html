Thorp's discipline — the rules we never break

Edward Thorp never risked capital without first proving the edge mathematically. He sized positions by the Kelly Criterion — never overbet, never underbet, always proportional to conviction and probability. He kept cash in reserve at all times. He defined his exit before he entered. He never chased a position that moved away from him.

At BlueLotus we apply this discipline to every order: cash reserve maintained at all times, stop loss 8% on all positions, 90% profit taken at target with 10% free rider, and no new positions until PCE clarifies the macro path on May 28.
