6. SCORING LOGIC
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Total score range:
  0 to 100

Higher score:
  stronger peace-dividend confirmation

Basket weights:
  Oil relief                     25
  Transport / airline benefit    20
  Consumer / inflation relief    15
  Safe-haven unwind              15
  Broad risk-on confirmation     15
  Defense cross-check             5

Headline confirmation:
  max +15

Escalation penalty:
  max -25

False-positive penalty:
  rule-driven, currently up to -20 for demand destruction and -12 for safe-haven
  conflict.

Status mapping:
  CONFIRMING    >= 75
  WATCH         >= 55
  MIXED         >= 40
  WEAKENING     >= 20
  CONTRADICTED  < 20
  UNKNOWN       insufficient data/null status path

Confidence logic:
  HIGH requires configured core basket confirmation:
    OIL_RISK_PREMIUM
    TRANSPORT_AIRLINES
    SAFE_HAVEN_UNWIND
    BROAD_RISK_ON

  MEDIUM requires configured minimum pass count.
  LOW requires partial confirmation.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. FALSE-POSITIVE LOGIC
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
False-positive rules are YAML-driven.

Demand destruction:
  If oil relief is PASS while broad risk, transports, and consumer baskets FAIL,
  S9 flags oil down due to demand weakness, not peace dividend.

Safe-haven conflict:
  If oil relief is PASS while safe-haven/volatility unwind FAILS, S9 flags mixed
  unresolved peace-dividend evidence.

Add permission is blocked when:
  - status is not CONFIRMING
  - confidence is not HIGH/MEDIUM
  - core required baskets do not pass
  - escalation penalty exceeds configured limit
  - false-positive penalty exceeds configured limit
  - S7/S8 external evidence blocks add

add_allowed=true means CIO review permission only, never trading permission.
