KC_03 US Recession Transmission (Score: 0.35 WATCH)
Canada's structural dependency on the US is existential: 75% of all Canadian exports flow south. A confirmed US recession would trigger Canadian GDP contraction within 2–3 quarters, forcing an emergency BOC rate cycle.
