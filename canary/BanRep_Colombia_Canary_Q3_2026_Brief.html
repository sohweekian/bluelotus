EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The BanRep Colombia Canary registers COKRI 0.3720 — WATCH as of Q3 2026. Colombia faces a complex quadruple pressure: Petro's fiscal expansion, oil revenue decline from no-new-licences policy, COP depreciation risk, and the world-unique security risk from FARC dissidents and ELN armed groups as economic variables.

The Ecopetrol production miss in January 2026 confirmed the oil revenue cliff risk. BanRep has delivered a full rate cut cycle from 13.25% peak to 5.75%, and Colombia CPI is approaching target. However, the fiscal deficit remains wide at -4.5% GDP, driven by pension reform costs and reduced oil tax revenues.

Pacific corridor significance: Cartagena is LatAm's largest container port. Colombia's Pacific coast position adjacent to the Panama Canal gives this canary unique trade disruption signal value.
