The Unique Denmark Mechanism — Why Denmark Is the Canary

NO MONETARY INDEPENDENCE

Danmarks Nationalbank has zero independent monetary policy. DKK must track ECB at 7.46038 DKK/EUR ±2.25%. Band: [7.29252, 7.62824]. FX reserves ~DKK 700B deployed to defend peg. Any housing correction arrives with no domestic rate lever to offset it.

OFFSHORE WIND SUPERPOWER

Ørsted (ORSTED.CO / DNNGY): world's largest offshore wind developer. Vestas (VWS.CO / VWSYF): world's largest wind turbine manufacturer. Both Danish. Both publicly listed. Denmark holds ~10% of global offshore wind capacity. Ørsted stock crashed 60%+ in 2023 on US write-downs.

WORLD'S HIGHEST HOUSEHOLD DEBT

Danish household debt at 280% of disposable income — the world's highest. House prices 40% above EU average (price-to-income 8.2x). A housing correction triggers demand collapse with no monetary offset. Totalkredit and Realkreditrådet are key stress monitors.

GLOBAL ENERGY TRANSITION CANARY

Denmark's Ørsted/Vestas complex makes it the first mover to register global offshore wind stress. US offshore wind write-downs hit Denmark first. Sector financing withdrawal shows in Ørsted stock before global peers. Denmark = the canary for energy transition viability.
